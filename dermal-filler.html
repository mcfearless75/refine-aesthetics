What is dermal filler?

Dermal filler uses hyaluronic acid (HA) — a substance that occurs naturally in the body — to restore volume, define contours and smooth deeper lines. Because HA filler is biodegradable and reversible, it has become one of the most trusted injectable treatments available.

At Refine Aesthetics in West Kirby, Sam approaches every filler consultation with precision and restraint. The goal is always balance and proportion — not obvious enhancement. She uses premium HA products suited to the specific area being treated and carries hyaluronidase (dissolver) at all times as a safety measure.

Sam treats clients from across Wirral including Hoylake, Heswall, Neston, Bebington, Birkenhead and West Kirby. A thorough consultation is always included before any treatment proceeds.
