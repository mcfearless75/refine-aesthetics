Skin treatments at Refine Aesthetics

Beyond injectables, Refine Aesthetics offers a range of medical-grade skin treatments designed to improve the condition, texture and luminosity of your skin from the surface down. Whether you're targeting skin laxity, dullness, pigmentation, acne scarring or simply want to maintain a healthy, glowing complexion — Sam will build a treatment plan around your specific skin concerns.

All treatments use professional-grade products and devices not available over the counter. Sam treats clients from across Wirral including Hoylake, Heswall, Neston and Birkenhead.

Available skin treatments

Injectable

Profhilo

Profhilo is a bio-remodelling injectable — not a filler — that delivers ultra-pure, high-concentration hyaluronic acid into the skin to deeply hydrate and trigger the production of collagen and elastin. The result is firmer, smoother, more luminous skin. Two sessions four weeks apart are typically recommended for the face, neck or hands.

From £220 per session
Injectable

Injectable Skin Boosters

A broad range of injectable skin-quality treatments including INFINI, Seventy Hyal, Jalupro, Jalutox and Polynucleotides. Each targets different skin concerns — from deep hydration and skin density to hair restoration and eye rejuvenation. Sam will advise on the right product for your skin at consultation.

From £140
Skin Renewal

Microneedling

Refine Aesthetics uses the EXCEED Precision Medical Microneedling system — a gold-standard device that creates precisely controlled micro-channels to stimulate skin renewal. Available for the face alone, face and neck, and with the addition of Exosomes for enhanced regeneration. Effective for fine lines, scarring, enlarged pores and uneven texture.

Face from £167  |  With Exosomes from £227
