What is lip filler and how does it work?

Lip filler uses hyaluronic acid (HA), a sugar molecule that occurs naturally in the body. When injected, HA attracts and retains water, adding volume and definition where placed. The effects are immediate and, unlike some older treatments, fully reversible with hyaluronidase if needed.

Samantha uses only premium, CE-marked HA products with integrated lidocaine to keep the treatment as comfortable as possible. The injections take roughly 20 to 30 minutes once topical anaesthetic has been applied. Most clients are back to their usual routine within the hour.

Who is lip filler suitable for?

Lip filler works well for a wide range of concerns. Common reasons clients from West Kirby, Hoylake, Heswall, Neston and across the Wirral Peninsula visit the clinic include:

  • Lips that have thinned with age and lost their natural fullness
  • An uneven or asymmetric lip shape
  • A flat or undefined cupid's bow
  • Loss of lip border definition
  • Adding subtle volume to naturally thin lips
  • Smoothing vertical lip lines around the mouth

A full consultation at Refine Aesthetics will establish whether lip filler is the right treatment for you, what volume is appropriate, and which product will give the best outcome for your anatomy. Samantha does not perform lip filler on clients who are pregnant, breastfeeding, or who have active cold sores or skin infections in the treatment area.

What to expect on the day

Your appointment starts with a review of your medical history and a detailed consultation. Samantha photographs the treatment area for your records and talks through the plan in full before proceeding.

Topical anaesthetic is applied and left to work for around 15 minutes. The filler is then placed using fine needles or a cannula depending on the area being treated. The whole process from consultation to completion is typically 45 to 60 minutes for a first appointment.

Aftercare is straightforward: avoid intense heat, alcohol and strenuous exercise for 24 hours, keep the area clean, and avoid pressing or massaging the lips for 48 hours. Samantha provides written aftercare instructions and a contact number for any questions in the days following treatment.
