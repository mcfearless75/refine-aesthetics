What are anti-wrinkle injections?

Anti-wrinkle injections use a purified form of botulinum toxin type A to temporarily relax the facial muscles responsible for expression lines. The result is a smoother, more rested appearance — without looking overdone or frozen.

At Refine Aesthetics in West Kirby, Sam takes a highly tailored approach. Every treatment begins with a thorough consultation to understand your concerns and your goals. The outcome should look like you on your best day — natural and refreshed.

Anti-wrinkle injections are one of the most popular aesthetic treatments in the UK and, when administered correctly by a qualified practitioner, carry an excellent safety record. Sam treats clients from across Wirral including Hoylake, Heswall, Neston, Bebington and beyond.

Frequently asked questions

Anti-wrinkle injections at Refine Aesthetics start from £150 for one area. Two areas cost £190 and three areas (full upper face) cost £230. Advanced treatments are priced on enquiry. All prices include a follow-up appointment.

Results typically last 3–4 months. With regular maintenance treatments, many clients find longevity improves slightly as the muscle learns to relax. Sam will advise on timing at your follow-up appointment.

Discomfort is minimal. The needles used are extremely fine and most clients describe the sensation as a small pinch. No anaesthetic is required for standard anti-wrinkle treatments.

Results appear gradually over 5–14 days as the muscle relaxes. You'll see the full effect at around the two-week mark, which is when your follow-up appointment is scheduled.
